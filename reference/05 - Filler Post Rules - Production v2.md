# Filler Post Generation Rules — Production v2

- AI-generated filler uses `isStoryPost: false`.
- Days 0–2 allow generic Escalate posts with blank reason fields.
- Day 3 unlocks the six parent reasons.
- Day 6 unlocks account metadata.
- Day 10 first expands Misinformation/Public Harm only.
- Day 13 introduces time pressure and harder cases.
- Use plausible names and places, not obvious placeholders.
- Real cities are acceptable; exact private addresses and real private people
  are not.
- Avoid real brands, named social platforms and branded product models.
- Use `[link removed]` instead of generated URLs.
- Political, anti-government and anti-AI opinions are allowed unless another
  violation is present.
- Dangerous challenges, text-only animal cruelty and deceptive impersonation
  are active design topics.
- Illegal drugs, weapons and regulated goods are planned under a future
  `IllegalRegulatedGoods` parent and remain inactive until supported.
- Copyright is excluded.
- Child-safety material is always excluded.
