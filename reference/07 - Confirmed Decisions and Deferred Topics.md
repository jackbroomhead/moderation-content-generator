# Confirmed Decisions and Deferred Topics

## Confirmed

### Parent escalation menu

Keep the original six reasons plus Needs Review. Future complexity branches from these parents rather than adding a large flat first-level list.

### Fraud

- obvious scam/phishing: Remove;
- complicated or uncertain suspected fraud: Escalate / `FraudScam`.

### Privacy

Sensitive personal information: Escalate / `PrivacySensitiveInfo`.

### Harassment and stalking

- playful/mutual teasing: Approve;
- one-off targeted bullying: Remove;
- sustained harassment, brigading or stalking: Escalate / `HarassmentAbuse`;
- use Privacy or Threat when that is the dominant harm.

### Misinformation and disinformation

- harmless myth, satire or opinion: Approve;
- clear harmful falsehood: Remove;
- uncertain, coordinated or potentially high-impact public harm: Escalate / `MisinformationPublicHarm`.

### Self-harm

- promotion/glorification without personal crisis: Remove;
- poster appears endangered or in crisis: Escalate / `SelfHarmCrisis`.

### Synthetic media

Start under Misinformation / Public Harm and later branch to a specialist challenge. The player must be given contextual evidence.

### Threats and graphic violence

- non-credible hyperbole: Approve;
- general encouragement of violence: Remove;
- credible/imminent threat: Escalate / `ThreatViolence`;
- graphic violence: Escalate through Threat / Violence until the later remove-plus-escalate workflow exists.

### Hate and extremism

Start broad and route through the existing parent representing the dominant harm. Later split into specialist branches to increase difficulty.

### Sexual content

- prohibited consensual adult explicit content: Remove;
- non-consensual imagery, nudify abuse or sextortion: Escalate using the dominant current parent.

### Child safety

Not included in the game and never generated.

### Spam

Ordinary spam: Remove. Coordinated manipulation is a later challenge routed by purpose until a specialist branch is implemented.

### Unsure

Future gameplay-only deferral. No payment; post returns later; no schema change.

## Deferred until a later policy pass

Do not generate these topics yet:

- illegal drugs and weapons sales;
- regulated goods;
- animal cruelty;
- copyright infringement;
- dangerous challenges;
- impersonation that does not involve fraud or synthetic media.
